# Installation on Cloud Provider

## Cloudron

Shipyard is available as a 1-click install on [Cloudron](https://cloudron.io).
Cloudron makes it easy to run apps like Shipyard on your server and keep them up-to-date and secure.

[![Install](/cloudron.svg)](https://cloudron.io/button.html?app=io.shipyard.cloudronapp)

The Shipyard package is maintained [here](https://git.cloudron.io/cloudron/shipyard-app).

There is a [demo instance](https://my.demo.cloudron.io) (username: cloudron password: cloudron) where
you can experiment with running Shipyard.

## Vultr

Shipyard can be found in [Vultr](https://www.vultr.com)'s marketplace.

To deploy Shipyard to Vultr, have a look at the [Vultr Marketplace](https://www.vultr.com/marketplace/apps/shipyard).

## DigitalOcean

[DigitalOcean](https://www.digitalocean.com) has Shipyard as droplet in their marketplace.

To deploy Shipyard to DigitalOcean, have a look at the [DigitalOcean Marketplace](https://marketplace.digitalocean.com/apps/shipyard).

## Linode

[Linode](https://www.linode.com/) has Shipyard as an app in their marketplace.

To deploy Shipyard to Linode, have a look at the [Linode Marketplace](https://www.linode.com/marketplace/apps/linode/shipyard/).

## alwaysdata

[alwaysdata](https://www.alwaysdata.com/) has Shipyard as an app in their marketplace.

To deploy Shipyard to alwaysdata, have a look at the [alwaysdata Marketplace](https://www.alwaysdata.com/en/marketplace/shipyard/).

## Exoscale

[Exoscale](https://www.exoscale.com/) provides Shipyard managed by [Glasskube](https://glasskube.eu/) in their marketplace.

Exoscale is a European cloud service provider.

The package is maintained and update via the open source [Glasskube Kubernetes Operator](https://github.com/glasskube/operator).

To deploy Shipyard to Exoscale, have a look at the [Exoscale Marketplace](https://www.exoscale.com/marketplace/listing/glasskube-shipyard/).
